# Privacy Policy for CacheFlux

**Effective Date:** June 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the **CacheFlux** browser extension (the "Extension"), created by Alberto Licea.

## 1. Information We Collect
**CacheFlux does not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell any user data.** 
The Extension operates entirely offline and locally within your browser. 

## 2. How Your Data is Handled
- **Site Data Clearing:** The Extension programmatically deletes cookies, local storage, session storage, CacheStorage, and IndexedDB data stored by the sites you visit.
- **Local Storage:** The Extension saves your personal configuration (such as your "Exception List" keys) locally in your browser using the standard browser storage API. This data never leaves your device.
- **Permissions:** 
  - `activeTab` and `<all_urls>` are used strictly to identify the current tab's domain so the Extension knows which site's data to clear.
  - `cookies`, `storage`, and `scripting` are used exclusively to perform the core functionality of deleting local data.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party tracking, analytics, or external telemetry services within the Extension.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's official repository.

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the official [GitHub repository](https://github.com/albertolicea00/cache-flux-addon).
