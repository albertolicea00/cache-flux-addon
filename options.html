CacheCleaner Settings

Configure cleanup preferences and protect specific data keys.

Select which categories of website data you want the cleaner to remove from the current page scope.

Choose actions to perform automatically after the cleaning process completes.

Define the exact names of cookies, LocalStorage, or SessionStorage keys that you want to KEEP when you click the broom icon (e.g. `auth_token`, `session_id`).

💡 Force Clean: To perform a Force Clean that ignores these exceptions and deletes everything for the current active tab, right-click the extension icon and select "Force Clean".
